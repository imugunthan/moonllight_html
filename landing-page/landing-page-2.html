THE ULTIMATE FANTASY EXPERIENCE

Fantasy Football is a game where you put your football knowledge to the test and compete against your friends and family. You will take on a new role as a fantasy football manager, build your football team, and strategize throughout the season to beat your competition.

Get Started

How does it work?

Fantasy football incorporates data from the live matches of the Premier League, Bundesliga, La Liga & Serie A and your job as fantasy manager is to build the team that will earn you the most points based on the players’ performances. This means that the more football you watch, the more you’ll succeed… isn’t that music to your ears?

ALWAYS STAY
in the know

Here at Moonllight, we will keep you informed and up-to-date so you can make the best decisions when it comes to making trades, selecting your starting 11, and strategizing week-over-week.

Get Started in
3 simple steps

01

Create a Fantasy League and customize the settings by choosing which leagues you and your friends want to play with.

02

Invite Other Fantasy Managers to compete against co-workers, friends, and family. Make it exciting and raise the stakes by creating winner incentives/loser punishments.

03

Set a Draft Date and Draft Your Players either in-person or online. If you really want to get into the drafting spirit, get everyone together to throw a draft party! Moonllight provides you with all the data to make your draft picks and build your team!

KEEP TRACK OF EVERYTHING

Throughout the season, you’ll have access to your fantasy league dashboard – a place where you’ll be able to view your fantasy league standings, gameweek info, and the top players of the week/season.

AN ENHANCED WAY TO VIEW FIXTURES

You may think that managing a team of players from multiple leagues is going to be challenging, but not with our enhanced gameweek calendar. You’ll be able to see exactly when the matches start/end for current, past, and future gameweeks as well as when the transfer window opens. The transfer window is where you’ll be able to trade with other managers and make your lineup adjustments.

EXPERIENCE EVERYTHING
in real time

Once the gameweek begins, your players will start earning points based on their match performances. Watch the scores update in real-time and definitely don’t forget to talk trash to your friends. As the season goes on, try out other features we have in the website like picking up players from the free market and making trades with other managers. Stay active during the Transfer Window periods in order to keep things fresh and stay on top of your game!
