GAME RULES

Automated Draft Picks

If you fail to pick a player within the time limit, then the system will automatically make a selection for you based on the following

Criteria:

1. Select the highest priority player from your watchlist who is available, within your budget, and in a position you still require.

2. If the players from your watchlist are no longer available, select the highest ranked player available in the draft list, within your budget, and in a position you still require.

If an automatic pick is made for you for 2 straight rounds, then you will be marked as being offline and picks will be automatically made in subsequent rounds. To resume manual drafting, go back to the draftroom and mark yourself as available.

Deadlines

The gameweek deadline is 10 minutes before the gameweek begins. So, the gameweek transfer window starts from when the previous gameweek ends to 10 minutes before the next gameweek begins. During this time, fantasy managers should set their lineups (starting 11, substitutions, and captains) and make any trades/transfers. After the deadline, these actions are prohibited until the next transfer window is open.

Trades/Wagers

After the draft has completed, you can trade players with other managers in your fantasy league.

Making a Trade Offer

Trade offers can be made each Gameweek until 10 minutes before the first pro match of the Gameweek, also known as the trade deadline.

A trade offer involves proposing to swap one or more players in your fantasy team (and/or trading budget in addition to players), with the same number of players from another fantasy team in your fantasy league. Trades can involve multiple players, but must follow fantasy team positional requirements (i.e. you cannot have more than 3 forwards). This is where utility players will become useful.
